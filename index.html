Experience Wilderness in Luxury

Tabibu Jay Safari Lodge offers an exclusive African safari experience combining luxury accommodation with authentic wildlife encounters. Nestled on a private concession bordering the Maasai Mara National Reserve, our lodge provides an intimate setting with unparalleled access to the Big Five.

Each of our luxury safari tents is designed to immerse you in nature while providing every modern comfort. Floor-to-ceiling mesh windows open onto private decks with panoramic views of the savannah, where you can watch wildlife from the privacy of your tent.

Our expert Maasai guides lead twice-daily game drives in custom safari vehicles, ensuring you have the best opportunities to witness the Great Migration, predator-prey interactions, and the diverse ecosystems of the Mara.
