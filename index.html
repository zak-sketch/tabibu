Experience Wilderness in Luxury

Tabibu Jay Safari Lodge offers an exclusive African safari experience combining luxury accommodation with authentic wildlife encounters. Nestled on a private concession bordering the Maasai Mara National Reserve, our lodge provides an intimate setting with unparalleled access to the Big Five.

Each of our eight luxury suites is designed to immerse you in nature while providing every modern comfort. Floor-to-ceiling glass doors open onto private decks with panoramic views of the savannah, where you can watch wildlife from the privacy of your room.

Our expert guides lead twice-daily game drives in custom safari vehicles, ensuring you have the best opportunities to witness the Great Migration, predator-prey interactions, and the diverse ecosystems of the Mara.

Prime Location in Maasai Mara

Our lodge is strategically located on a private 50,000-acre conservancy bordering the Maasai Mara National Reserve. This privileged position allows exclusive access to wildlife viewing without the crowds, while still being close to the main reserve for witnessing the Great Migration.

Access Information

We're accessible via scheduled flights from Nairobi Wilson Airport to our private airstrip (45-minute flight). Transfers from the airstrip to the lodge take approximately 20 minutes.
